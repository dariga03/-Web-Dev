Recipe #1 "Pizza"

  • Time: 2hour
  • Serves: Pizza
Ingredients Quantity
flour 330g
water 170ml
Yeasts 4g
oil 10 ml
tomato 3 pieces
cheese 350g
sauce 250g

Step 1:To make the pizza dough, first sift the flour. Make a funnel in the flour. Add yeast. Then add oil and water in small portions. At the same time, mix the flour from the edge to the center.

Step 2:The dough came together in a lump. Add salt. Knead for another 10 minutes.

Step 3:After rolling out the dough with your hands and spread the sauce ready.

Step 4:Chop the tomatoes and cheese through a grater.

Step 5:Then we send the dough to the oven with 180 degrees for 15 minutes.

Recipe #2 "Rice"

  • Time: 15m
  • Serves: Rice
Ingredients Quantity
rice 300g
water 150ml
salt 15g
oil 10 ml

Step 1:Pour water into a saucepan.

Step 2:We also send rice , salt and oil there.

Step 3:Set the heat to maximum until the water boils and leave the pan open.

Step 4:When the water boils, immediately set the fire to a minimum and wait for 10 minutes exactly.

Recipe #3 "Omelet"

  • Time: 10m
  • Serves: Omelet
Ingredients Quantity
egg 2pieces
oil 5ml
salt 5g
tomato 1pieces

Step 1:First, cut the tomatoes.

Step 2:Break the eggs into a container and mix with salt.

Step 3:Grease the pan with oil and pour the mixed egg into it.

Step 4:After a minute, put the chopped tomatoes there and fry for 5 minutes, and on the other side for 5 minutes.
